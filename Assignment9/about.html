About This Project

I really enjoy tie-dye and find it really relaxing. This tutorial site aims to share how easy and enjoyable tie-dyeing can be, making it accessible to everyone.

Why Tie-Dye?

Tie-dye is a wonderful combination of art and science, allowing everyone to create unique pieces while learning about color theory and fabric manipulation.
